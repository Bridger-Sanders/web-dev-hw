Building a Puppet

Materials

  • Fabric (i.e. Fleece)
  • Foam (i.e. Polyfoam)
  • Fabric Shears
  • Razor Blades
  • Contact Cement
  • Needles & Thread
  • Sewing Pins
  • A Metal Wire Hanger
  • Puppet Pattern
  • Chalk Pencil
  • Respirator
  • Disposable Gloves

    • Making the Puppet

    1. Fabric

      1. Find a puppet pattern online or make one from scratch.
      2. Use pattern and the chalk penicl to label and outline the fabric.
      3. Use the fabric shears to cut the outline you made on the fabric.
      4. Use pins to hold the freshly cut pieces of fabric together, then commence sewing them together.
      5. Be sure not to sew the bottom where your hand is suppose to go.

    2. Foam

      1. Using the puppet pattern and chalk pencil, outline and label the foam.
      2. Use your razors to cut the outline of the foam.
      3. Dispose of dull razors by putting them in a small container, then throw it in the garbage.
      4. Put on a respirator and disposable gloves before using the contact cement to glue the designated foam pieces together.
      5. Glue both sides for extra reinforcement.
      6. Wait a few minutes before pressing the freshly glued foam pieces together.
      7. Once dried, put the glue pressed foam into your puppet.

    3. Stand & Decoration

      1. Puppet Stand
        • Glue a cardboard shipping tube to a flat square wooden board.
        • Screw a thin sturdy wooden rod to a flat square wooden board.
        • Buy a stand.
      2. Arms
        • For opposable fingers use a wire of some kind, hence the wire hanger.
        • Take care to sew the arms to the puppet thoroughly.
      3. Hair
        • Yarn
        • Fur Scraps
        • Wigs
      4. Eyes
        • Ping Pong Balls
        • Buttons
        • Easter Eggs
        • Doll Eyes
      5. Clothes
        • Look for toddler size clothing patterns.
        • Buy actual toddler size clothing.
      6. Other
        • Wool Roving
        • Get Creative
