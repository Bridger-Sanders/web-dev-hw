Protein Bars

The source of the protein bar recipe is on DAREBEE's website.

Ingredients

  • Four and a half cups uncooked oatmeal
  • One cup peanut butter
  • One cup coconut cream/milk
  • Three cups whey vanilla protein powder

Directions

  1. Whisk the coconut cream/milk in a large bowl until smooth.
  2. Put the protein powder in the same large bowl and commence whisking.
  3. Put the peanut butter in next and mix it all with a spoon.
  4. Pour the uncooked oatmeal into the large bowl and mix with your hands.
  5. Flatten mix into a dish/baking sheet lined with foil/baking paper.
  6. Throw that mix in the fridge to chill out for two hours or more, then cut the mix into bars.
